Exploring Brainwave Patterns through Frequency Analysis

EEG Analysis Overview

This project involved analyzing EEG data to identify patterns in different brainwave frequencies. By examining the Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta, and Gamma bands, we gained insights into cognitive states such as relaxation, deep sleep, and active thinking.
